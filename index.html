Climate Change

Climate change is a long-term alteration of temperature and typical weather patterns. It is primarily caused by human activities such as deforestation, industrial emissions, and the burning of fossil fuels.

Effects of Climate Change

  • Rising sea levels
  • Increased natural disasters
  • Melting glaciers and Arctic ice
  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Extreme weather conditions

Real World Examples

From the melting Himalayan glaciers to wildfires in Australia, climate change is evident across the globe. The 2023 floods in Pakistan and increasing droughts in Africa are just a few examples of its devastating impacts.

How Can We Help?

  • Switch to renewable energy sources
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle
  • Use public transportation and eco-friendly alternatives
  • Support climate-friendly policies
  • Raise awareness in your community

Conclusion
